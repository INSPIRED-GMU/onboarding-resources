## Installing

Make sure you [install the font](https://github.com/tonsky/FiraCode/wiki/Installing).

## Enabling

In RStudio:
- Go to Tools > Global Options > Appearance
- Select "Fira Code" as Editor Font. In older versions of RStudio, check "Use Ligatures". 
- Hit "OK" and enjoy
